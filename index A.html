Conclusion & Future Directions

Key Insights

  • TF-IDF proved to be a highly effective feature engineering technique for this task.
  • Simple models like Logistic Regression and Naive Bayes can serve as strong, efficient baselines for sentiment analysis.
  • A balanced dataset is crucial for training an unbiased classifier.

Future Enhancements

  • Explore advanced embeddings like Word2Vec or GloVe to capture semantic meaning.
  • Implement deep learning models such as LSTMs or transformers (BERT) for potentially higher accuracy.
  • Conduct hyperparameter tuning to further optimize model performance.
  • Improve preprocessing to better handle nuances like negation (e.g., "not good").
