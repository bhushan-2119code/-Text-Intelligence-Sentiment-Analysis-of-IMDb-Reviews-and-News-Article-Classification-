Automatic News Classification

An interactive report on teaching machines to understand text.

From Raw Text to Intelligent Insights

Have you ever wondered how your news feed knows exactly what you're interested in? The answer is a powerful technology called text classification. This report details a project to build a machine learning model that automatically reads a news article and classifies it into one of three categories: Technology, Sports, or Politics. We'll explore the data, compare different models, dive into the results, and even let you try the classifier yourself.
